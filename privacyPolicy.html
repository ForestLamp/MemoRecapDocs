1. Data Storage Architecture

The Application does not use its own backend servers to store user content.

All user-generated data, including:

  • Voice recordings
  • Transcripts
  • AI-generated summaries
  • Custom AI prompts and instructions

is stored locally on your device and/or in your personal iCloud account (if synchronization is enabled).

We do not have technical access to, and cannot retrieve, view, or restore user content. You are solely responsible for managing backups and iCloud synchronization.

2. Data We Collect and Process

2.1 User Content

When you use the Application, you create and store:

  • Voice recordings
  • Transcripts (generated from your recordings)
  • AI-generated summaries
  • Custom AI prompts and instructions

This content is processed solely to provide Application functionality and is never accessed, stored, or used by us for any other purpose.

2.2 Analytics Data

We use Firebase Analytics (provided by Google LLC) to improve the Application.

Firebase automatically collects:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version (iOS version)
  • App usage patterns (which features you use, how often)
  • Screen views and navigation paths
  • Crash logs and error reports
  • Anonymized device identifiers (IDFV, Analytics App Instance ID)
  • Approximate location (country/region level only)

What Firebase does NOT collect:

  • Your voice recordings
  • Transcripts or AI-generated content
  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Precise location data

Analytics data is retained by Firebase for 14 months, then automatically deleted.

Learn more: Firebase Privacy Policy

2.3 Subscription and Payment Data

All payments are processed exclusively by Apple Inc. through the App Store.

We never see or store:

  • Credit card numbers
  • Billing addresses
  • Payment method details

We use AppHud (provided by AppHud Inc.) to validate subscription status. AppHud only receives:

  • Subscription tier (e.g., "Pro Monthly")
  • Subscription status (active/expired)
  • Expiration date
  • Anonymous transaction identifiers

AppHud retains this data for the duration of your subscription plus 12 months for billing dispute resolution.

Learn more: AppHud Privacy Policy

3. How We Use Your Data

3.1 AI Processing

When you use transcription or summary features, your voice recordings and/or text are temporarily transmitted to Replicate, Inc. for AI processing.

How it works:

  • You tap "Transcribe" or "Summarize"
  • Your recording/text is sent to Replicate's servers
  • Replicate runs AI models (like Whisper for transcription)
  • Results are sent back to your device
  • Your original recording stays on your device

Data retention by Replicate:

  • Inputs and outputs may be retained for up to 30 days for debugging and abuse prevention
  • After 30 days, data is automatically deleted
  • Replicate does not use your data to train AI models

Learn more: Replicate Privacy Policy

3.2 Analytics and Improvement

We use Firebase Analytics data to:

  • Understand which features are most popular
  • Identify and fix crashes or bugs
  • Improve app performance and stability
  • Make decisions about future features

3.3 What We Do NOT Do With Your Data

We never:

  • Sell or rent your data to third parties
  • Use your recordings to train AI models
  • Share your content with advertisers
  • Access your voice recordings or transcripts
  • Track you across other apps or websites
  • Build profiles for targeted advertising
