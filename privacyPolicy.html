3. AI Processing

3.1 Third-Party AI Providers

When you use transcription or summary features, your recording or text is temporarily transmitted to Replicate, Inc. via encrypted TLS connection.

  • Processing occurs only when you actively request transcription or summarization.
  • We do not store AI inputs or outputs on our servers.
  • We do not authorize the use of your data for AI model training.

According to Replicate’s policies, inputs and outputs may be retained temporarily (for example, up to 30 days) for debugging, abuse prevention, and operational purposes, after which they are deleted in accordance with their policies.

AI outputs are generated automatically by third-party systems beyond our direct control.

AI-generated transcripts and summaries may contain inaccuracies or errors and are provided for informational purposes only. They should not be relied upon for medical, legal, financial, or professional decisions.

You may customize AI prompts and instructions. You are solely responsible for the content and configuration of custom prompts and for verifying AI-generated outputs before relying on them. We do not review, monitor, or control user-defined prompts.
